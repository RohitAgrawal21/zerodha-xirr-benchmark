How to use

  1. Download your cash ledger: Log in to Kite → Click Console (top-right) → Account → Ledger → Set date range (start from account opening) → Click CSV to download
  2. Upload the CSV(s) below. If you have multiple Zerodha accounts, upload each ledger separately
  3. Enter your holdings value — the current market value shown on your Kite dashboard
  4. Click Calculate XIRR to see how you did vs major indices
